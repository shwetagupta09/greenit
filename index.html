Green computing, green IT, or ICT sustainability,
is the study and practice of environmentally
sustainable computing or IT.
The goals of green computing are similar to
green chemistry: reduce the use of hazardous
materials, maximize energy efficiency during the
product's lifetime, the recyclability or
biodegradability of defunct products and factory
waste. Green computing is important for all
classes of systems, ranging from handheld
systems to large-scale data centers.
